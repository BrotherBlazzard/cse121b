Dice Game

Game Rules

  • Roll the Dice
  • 1s are worth 100 points
  • 5s are worth 50 points
  • If you roll a 1 or 5, you can choose to keep your points and walk away, or risk them all to roll again.
  • If you roll a hand without a 1 or 5, you lose all your points and lose the game.
  • See if you can reach 1,000 points!
